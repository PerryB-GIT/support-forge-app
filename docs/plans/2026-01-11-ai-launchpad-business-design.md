**What makes us different:**

- We teach the "why and how," not just the "what"

## Product Tiers

### Tier 1: Launchpad Starter
**Price:** $97/month or $497 one-time

**Includes:**
- Self-paced video course (the full training curriculum)
- "Golden config" templates and plugin recommendations
- Setup guides for GitHub, AWS free tier, Zapier, N8N
- Community access (Discord) for peer support
- Monthly group Q&A call

**Target:** DIY learners, price-sensitive, want to figure it out themselves

---

### Tier 2: Launchpad Pro
**Price:** $2,500 - $4,000

**Includes:**
- Everything in Starter
- 2-week structured cohort program (4-6 people per cohort)
- Live group sessions walking through setup
- Hands-on: Claude Code configured during the session
- Direct Slack/Discord access during the 2 weeks
- "Graduation" = fully set up and trained

**Target:** People who want guidance but don't need full custom work

---

### Tier 3: Launchpad Enterprise
**Price:** $10,000+

**Includes:**
- Full custom engagement
- 1-on-1 setup, training, deployment
- Ongoing support retainer option
- Integration with existing tools/workflows
- White-glove service for complex needs

**Target:** Businesses with budget, complex needs, or no time to DIY
